What is TechBlooms?

TechBlooms is an EdTech platform that bridges the gap between online learning and offline experience. We offer premium tech courses that you can purchase online and attend in person at India's top IIT campuses.

Our mission is to make world-class tech education accessible to everyone. We believe that learning technology shouldn't be limited by geography or resources.

Unlike purely online platforms, we provide hands-on learning experience at prestigious IIT campuses, giving you exposure to the best faculty, infrastructure, and peer network.
